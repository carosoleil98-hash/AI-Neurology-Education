# NeuroVisual Academy

### Open-Source Neurology Education Powered by AI

An open educational initiative dedicated to creating high-quality neurology learning resources through artificial intelligence, medical expertise, and visual storytelling.

This project provides educational content for medical students, residents, neurologists, neuroscientists, and healthcare professionals worldwide.

---

## Mission

Neurology is often considered one of the most challenging specialties to learn because many disease mechanisms occur at microscopic, molecular, and immunological levels.

Our mission is to transform complex neurological concepts into intuitive visual experiences using:

- AI-generated animations
- Medical illustrations
- Interactive learning materials
- Clinical case discussions
- Evidence-based educational content

We aim to make neurology easier to understand, teach, and communicate.
